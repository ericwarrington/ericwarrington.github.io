Curveball: 3D Pong

Project Dates: February 2015 - Present

Team Size: 1

Language: C# (Using Unity engine)

A game of Pong in 3D made with the Unity game engine. Beat your opponent by curving the ball with your paddle and bouncing it off the walls and past his paddle! Easy and fun to play, but difficult to master.

Features:

  • Control the curvature of the ball using your paddle's momentum!
  • Rip serves past your opponent with the unique serving mechanism!
  • Beat increasingly-difficult levels of AI, all the while trying to maximize your high-score in Infinite mode!
  • See what level you can reach with only one life in Sudden-Death mode!
  • Play quick matches with customizable AI levels in Battle mode!

Planned Features

  • Online multiplayer
  • Public high score list
  • Customizable/Challenge modes

Scoring for Infinite mode is based on:

  • Ball velocity
  • Ball rotational-velocity
  • How long the ball has been in play
  • The opponent's level
  • Your score streak (Score streaks at higher levels effect your score more than streaks at low levels)
  • The ratio of points lost/points won (over the current level and over the entire game)
  • The number of lives you have remaining

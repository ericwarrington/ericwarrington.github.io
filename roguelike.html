Roguelike

Project Dates: November 2015 - Present

Team Size: 1

Language: Swift (using SpriteKit)

A top-down dungeon-crawler roguelike game, designed to play and feel like The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth, yet be totally different at the same time.

Disclaimer

This is currently a game prototype/personal project. The current graphics (depicted in the screenshots) are not final. The enemies are placeholder "programmer art" and the walls and pickups will be redone before release. I have no intention of releasing as-is.

Source of copyrighted graphics:

  • The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past; reasoning: TBoI was based off the Zelda games, and I really like the style of LttP walls
  • Tibia (via this page); reasoning: I actually stumbled onto these via Google search, but they also match exactly what I was looking for in pickup sprites
  • Ray Wenderlich's SpriteKit tutorials

Description

Originally a project created for my iOS Game & Media App Development class--due December 2015--this roguelike game is something I've been periodically coming back to and improving on since. I made the choice to use an existing design, rather than creating an original one, so I could give my full attention to learning Swift/SpriteKit and improving my programming skills. I also find the way Rebirth uses the Random Number Generator (RNG) to determine enemies, items, and drops fascinating--something I wanted to try to mimic and maybe improve. In Rebirth, everything is dropped or generated randomly, so you could end up with an over-powered item early on and face easy bosses, or you could get screwed and get garbage items and be forced to face really tough bosses.
